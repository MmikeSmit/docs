You can enter copy trading from two main places:

- In the Smart Money list, click **Copy Trade** on the target wallet.
- On the wallet details page, review the trading behavior and click **Copy Trade**.

If you enter from Smart Money or wallet details, the lead wallet address is filled automatically.  
If you create a rule from the Copy Trading page in Profile, you need to enter the lead wallet address manually.

## Creation flow

1. Open the target wallet's copy trading panel.
2. Confirm the lead wallet address.
3. Choose a copy mode: Smart Copy or Instant Copy.
4. Enter the total allocation.
5. Confirm available balance, minimum allocation, and possible activation fee.
6. Click **Copy Trade** to create the rule.
7. After creation, go to **Profile -> Copy Trading** to view and manage the rule.

<Info>
Copy trading rules are processed asynchronously by the backend. A successful creation does not mean every position is synced immediately. Page data may take a short time to update.
</Info>
