The Invites and Points module shows referral relationships, reward status, and campaign points.

## Invites

Users can share their invitation link or referral code. After an invited user completes binding, the referral relationship appears on the related page.

Common information includes:

- referral code
- invite link
- invited users
- reward status

## Points

Points record user participation in campaigns. Users can view current points, point history, and campaign-related rewards.

<Info>
Reward rules may change by campaign. Always follow the page display and official announcements.
</Info>
