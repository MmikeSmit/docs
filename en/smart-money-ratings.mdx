## Trading rating filters

The Smart Money list includes **Trading Rating** filters:

- Conservative Risk
- Balanced
- Aggressive Return

These are system-generated groups based on the wallet's trading performance during the selected time range. They help narrow the candidate list quickly. They are not user-created labels and they are not permanent identity tags. When you switch between 1 day, 7 days, 30 days, or all-time data, the rating can change with the underlying period.

| Rating | What it helps screen for |
| --- | --- |
| Conservative Risk | Wallets that lean toward risk control and stable performance. Useful when you want candidates with relatively controlled drawdown and less aggressive trade pacing. |
| Balanced | Wallets with a more balanced relationship between return and risk. Useful when you want to avoid both overly defensive and overly aggressive traders. |
| Aggressive Return | Wallets that lean toward return-seeking behavior. These usually require closer checks on drawdown, leverage, current positions, and recent style changes. |

The filter supports multi-select. For example, selecting both Conservative Risk and Balanced returns wallets that match either rating. Adding account size, market preference, directional bias, or other filters further narrows the result set.
