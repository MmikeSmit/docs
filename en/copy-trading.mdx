Copy trading lets you use your own capital to follow a lead wallet's trading behavior. SeaBond assigns a dedicated copy trading account and shows copy trading equity, rule status, PnL, and history separately in Profile.

In SeaBond, copy trading is not mixed with trades from your main wallet. Your main account and copy trading account are shown separately so you can understand where profit, loss, and risk are coming from.
