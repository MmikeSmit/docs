Do not look only at total PnL. Review these metrics together:

- realized PnL
- unrealized PnL
- number of current copy trading positions
- account value
- maintenance margin ratio
- win rate of closed positions
- start time and end time

These metrics help you decide whether the rule is still within your risk range.

## Risk notes

Copy trading is affected by market volatility, lead wallet behavior, slippage, execution delay, insufficient margin, and liquidation risk.

<Warning>
SeaBond can help you create and manage copy trading rules, but it cannot guarantee that the lead wallet will profit or that your result will match the lead wallet exactly.
</Warning>
