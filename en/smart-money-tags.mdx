Use these tags to decide whether the wallet matches your copy trading preference before opening the details page.

### Account size criteria

Account size is based on current position exposure:

| Icon | Tag | Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/whale.svg" alt="Whale" width="18" /> | Whale | Position exposure above 1,000,000 USDC |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/shark.svg" alt="Shark" width="18" /> | Shark | Position exposure from 100,000 to 1,000,000 USDC |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/dolphin.svg" alt="Dolphin" width="18" /> | Dolphin | Position exposure from 50,000 to 100,000 USDC |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/fish.svg" alt="Fish" width="18" /> | Fish | Position exposure from 10,000 to 50,000 USDC |

This tag helps estimate wallet scale. Larger wallets usually reflect more meaningful capital behavior. Smaller wallets can show inflated percentage returns from only a few trades.

### Performance marker criteria

Performance markers highlight accounts with notable recent trading behavior:

| Icon | Tag | Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/on-fire.svg" alt="Win-rate Hunter" width="18" /> | Win-rate Hunter | Win rate above 80% and more than 60 opened trades in the last 30 days |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/shield.svg" alt="Steady Compounder" width="18" /> | Steady Compounder | Profitable for more than six months and more than 30 opened trades in the last 30 days |
| <img src="/assets/smart-money/lightning.svg" alt="Profit Machine" width="18" /> | Profit Machine | Win rate above 60% and more than 300 opened trades in the last 30 days |

These markers only mean the wallet meets the data condition. They do not guarantee future profitability. Before following, also review current positions, drawdown, leverage, and traded markets.
