## Wallet profile tags

Wallet cards show system-generated profile tags beside the wallet avatar. These tags summarize the wallet's trading characteristics. They are not manual favorite labels; they are generated from wallet data.

Common tags can be read in four groups:

### Account size

Used to classify wallet scale by current position exposure.

| Tag | Meaning | Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/whale.svg" alt="Whale" />Whale | Very large current position exposure. | Position exposure above 1,000,000 USDC |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/shark.svg" alt="Shark" />Shark | Large current position exposure. | Position exposure from 100,000 to 1,000,000 USDC |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/dolphin.svg" alt="Dolphin" />Dolphin | Medium current position exposure. | Position exposure from 50,000 to 100,000 USDC |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/fish.svg" alt="Fish" />Fish | Smaller current position exposure. | Position exposure from 10,000 to 50,000 USDC |

### Market preference

Used to identify what types of markets the wallet mainly trades.

| Tag | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/majors.svg" alt="Majors" />Majors | More often trades BTC, ETH, and other major markets. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/alts.svg" alt="Alts" />Alts | More often trades higher-volatility alternative markets. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/tradfi.svg" alt="TradFi" />TradFi | More often trades traditional-finance-related markets. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/mixed.svg" alt="Mixed" />Mixed | Trades across multiple market types without one dominant preference. |

### Directional bias

Used to show whether the wallet more often takes long, short, or balanced exposure.

| Tag | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/long-biased.svg" alt="Long-biased" />Long-biased | More often holds long exposure. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/short-biased.svg" alt="Short-biased" />Short-biased | More often holds short exposure. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/hybrid.svg" alt="Balanced" />Balanced | Long and short exposure are relatively balanced. |

### Holding period and performance markers

Used to identify trading tempo and standout recent behavior.

| Tag | Meaning | Criteria |
| --- | --- | --- |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/trend.svg" alt="Scalping" />Scalping | More short-frequency trading. | Determined by holding period and trading tempo. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/short-term.svg" alt="Short-term" />Short-term | More short holding periods. | Determined by holding period and trading tempo. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/high-freq.svg" alt="Medium-term" />Medium-term | More medium holding periods. | Determined by holding period and trading tempo. |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/on-fire.svg" alt="Win-rate Hunter" />Win-rate Hunter | Standout win-rate behavior. | Win rate above 80% and more than 60 opened trades in the last 30 days |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/shield.svg" alt="Steady Compounder" />Steady Compounder | More stable long-period performance. | Profitable for more than six months and more than 30 opened trades in the last 30 days |
| <Icon src="/assets/smart-money/lightning.svg" alt="Profit Machine" />Profit Machine | Standout activity and profitability. | Win rate above 60% and more than 300 opened trades in the last 30 days |

For example, **Balanced / Long-biased / Short-term** means the wallet is relatively balanced across long and short exposure overall, has recently leaned more long, and tends to trade on a shorter time frame. Use these tags to decide whether the wallet matches your copy trading preference before opening the details page.

Account size helps estimate wallet scale. Larger wallets usually reflect more meaningful capital behavior. Smaller wallets can show inflated percentage returns from only a few trades.

Performance markers only mean the wallet meets the data condition. They do not guarantee future profitability. Before following, also review current positions, drawdown, leverage, and traded markets.
