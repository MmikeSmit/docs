Profile is where you review account status and copy trading results. After finding wallets in Smart Money and reviewing wallet details, you come back here to see where your capital is, how copy trading is performing, and whether your main account has risk exposure.

## When to use Profile

You usually open Profile when you need to:

- check copy trading account performance after creating a rule
- confirm changes in total account value
- review main wallet positions, open orders, and order history
- view deposit, withdrawal, or trade history
- update avatar, display name, or bio

## Overview: start with the whole account

Overview answers one question first: how is my account doing overall?

It combines your main wallet and copy trading accounts, and is useful for checking:

- estimated total assets
- 7-day PnL
- asset distribution
- deposit and withdrawal records
- profile entry

If you only want a fast account-level view, start with Overview.

## Copy Trading: review copy accounts only

The Copy Trading page only shows copy trading accounts. It does not mix in trades from your main wallet.

Use it to check:

- copy trading equity
- which rules are still active
- current copy trading PnL
- whether there is unrealized PnL
- historical copy trading performance

<Warning>
After creating, closing, or adjusting a copy trading rule, data may not sync immediately. Short-term status may still be updating.
</Warning>

## Unified Trading: review your own trading account

Unified Trading shows the trading account for your main wallet.

It is useful for reviewing:

- current positions
- spot assets
- fill history
- open orders
- funding rate records
- historical orders

If you want to separate your own trading PnL from copy trading PnL, compare Unified Trading with Copy Trading.

## My Profile: manage personal information

Open the profile entry to update personal information and view account level.

You can manage:

- avatar
- display name
- UID
- VIP level
- bio
- fee information

## How to avoid mixing data

Remember these three entries:

- **Overview**: main wallet + copy trading accounts
- **Copy Trading**: copy trading accounts only
- **Unified Trading**: your main wallet trading account only

This helps you identify whether PnL comes from your own trading or from copy trading.
