SeaBond provides trading data analysis, wallet discovery, and copy trading assistance. It does not provide investment advice.

## Trading risk

Digital asset and perpetual futures trading involve high risk. Market prices can move quickly. Leveraged trading can lead to insufficient margin, liquidation, or loss of principal.

## Copy trading risk

Copy trading does not guarantee profit. Even if a lead wallet performed well in the past, it can lose money in the future.

Copy trading risks include:

- losses from the lead wallet
- slippage and execution delay
- different entry prices
- different capital size
- different leverage and margin state
- changes in the lead wallet's trading style
- fast market movement
- liquidation risk

## User responsibility

Users should decide whether to trade or copy trade based on their own judgment and risk tolerance.

SeaBond does not guarantee any wallet's performance and does not guarantee that your copy trading result will match the lead wallet exactly.

<Warning>
Only use capital you can afford to lose for trading or copy trading.
</Warning>
